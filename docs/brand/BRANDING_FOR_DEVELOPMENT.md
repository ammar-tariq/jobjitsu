# Branding Rules for Development

> How JobJitsu brand principles drive product and engineering decisions.

Cursor enforces these via `.cursor/rules/branding-development.mdc` (always on).

---

## Principles

1. **Never expose raw AI terminology unless useful.** Prefer agent, on-device, draft, review. Keep LLM/model/prompt language for advanced settings or engineering docs.
2. **Say "Agent" instead of "LLM" in the UI.** Status chrome: **Agent · On-device**, not Local LLM.
3. **Always reinforce local-first privacy.** “Stored on this device.” “No data leaves this device.”
4. **Use calm, encouraging language.** Coach voice — see [VOICE_AND_TONE.md](./VOICE_AND_TONE.md).
5. **Never pressure the user.** No FOMO, streaks, guilt, or urgency theater.
6. **Human approval is required before external actions.** Agent prepares; Send requires the user.
7. **Keep interfaces clean and uncluttered.** One job per view; no cockpit sprawl.
