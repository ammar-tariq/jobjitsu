# JobJitsu Product Vision

> **JobJitsu is an AI Career Operating System.**  
> The gentle art of landing the job — and building a career you own.  
> On-device. On-target. On your terms.

Companion docs: [PRODUCT_PHILOSOPHY.md](./PRODUCT_PHILOSOPHY.md) · [BRAND_GUIDELINES.md](./BRAND_GUIDELINES.md) · [VOICE_AND_TONE.md](./VOICE_AND_TONE.md)

This document defines **where the product is going**. It is not an implementation plan, API design, or engineering backlog.

---

## Vision

**Every person deserves a private, local AI system that runs their career craft with the same calm precision a black belt brings to the mat.**

JobJitsu becomes the **AI Career Operating System**: a desktop-native, open-source environment where your résumé, preferences, agent, and local LLM form a single dojo for the full career loop — discover, decide, apply, follow up, learn, and grow — without renting your data to the cloud.

In that future:

- Career work feels like **technique**, not spam or panic.
- The agent is always **yours** — ready when your belt is tied, still when you pause.
- Nothing intimate leaves the machine except what you **explicitly send**.
- Progress is quiet, measurable, and free of urgency theater.
- Trust comes from **open source + on-device architecture**, not from a privacy policy slide.

JobJitsu is not “ChatGPT for jobs.” It is the operating layer for a career practiced with leverage.

---

## Mission

**Give job seekers and career builders a local AI black belt for the entire hunt — and, over time, for the wider craft of building a career — so they can act with precision, privacy, and control.**

We exist to:

1. Replace spray-and-pray and cloud résumé farms with **on-device, preference-driven craft**.
2. Keep the human in command: the agent prepares; **you approve** high-stakes sends.
3. Surface the pipeline calmly — queue, applications, follow-ups — without a cockpit of anxiety.
4. Stay open-source, inspectable, and free of subscription traps tied to holding career data hostage.

**Tagline alignment**

| Promise | Meaning in the OS |
|---------|-------------------|
| On-device | Local agent + local LLM; privacy as architecture |
| On-target | Technique over volume; fit over force |
| On your terms | Preferences, approvals, pause — user sovereignty |

## Long-term Roadmap

A directional arc, not a release schedule. Each horizon deepens the Career OS without abandoning the brand.

### Horizon 1 — The Application Dojo *(foundation)*
Establish JobJitsu as the trusted local system for the classic pipeline:

**search → curate → tailor → queue → approve → send → follow up**

Outcome: a calm desktop companion with visible Local LLM status, user-owned agent, and mastery-over-volume defaults.

### Horizon 2 — The Full Hunt Loop *(depth)*
Close the loop after the throw: outcomes, notes, timelines, and gentle learning from what worked — still on-device, still without FOMO metrics.

Outcome: one coherent OS for “what I sent, what I nudged, what I learned,” not a pile of disconnected tools.

### Horizon 3 — Career Craft Beyond Apply *(breadth)*
Extend the same philosophy into adjacent craft surfaces (interview readiness, narrative coherence, role fit reflection) as **modules inside the same dojo** — never as a separate hype product or cloud upsell.

Outcome: JobJitsu is credibly an **AI Career Operating System**, not only an auto-apply utility.

### Horizon 4 — Sovereign Ecosystem *(enduring)*
An open, extensible local career OS: community-visible agents/skills, optional bridges only when the user initiates outbound action, identity intact — calm, private, craft-first.

Outcome: the default choice for people who refuse to outsource their career data.

```
Horizon 1          Horizon 2           Horizon 3            Horizon 4
Application   →    Full hunt loop  →   Career craft    →    Sovereign
Dojo               (outcomes +         beyond apply         ecosystem
                   learning)           (modular depth)
```

## Core Modules

These are the **spine** of the Career OS. They must feel like one product, one voice, one privacy model.

| Module | Purpose | Brand posture |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Identity & Resume** | On-device profile and résumé as source of truth for tailoring | Stored locally; never implied to be “synced to JobJitsu cloud” |
| **Preferences** | Rules for fit, tone, constraints, approval gates | “Your rules, your signal” |
| **Local Intelligence** | Local LLM + embeddings/context for understanding roles and drafting | Status always visible (“Local LLM”); warm-up copy stays honest |
| **Agent** | Executes prep work against preferences; pauses on command | Serves; does not own. Idle: belt tied, waiting |
| **Discovery & Curation** | Find and filter roles toward fit | Precision first, volume second |
| **Applications** | Draft, tailor, version, and track each throw | Craft unit of the OS |
| **Queue & Review** | Holding area before anything leaves the machine | Approval is a first-class ritual |
| **Send** | Explicit outbound apply / submit | User-initiated; success is a quiet nod |
| **Follow-ups** | Polite nudges on a calm calendar | Amber caution, never shame |
| **Timeline & Memory** | Local history of actions and outcomes | Learning without leaderboard energy |
| **Privacy & Trust Chrome** | Badge, logs, clear “what left / what stayed” | Architecture made visible |

Together these modules fulfill the Leverage Belt metaphor: **search → curate → apply → send → follow up**, under one operating system.

## Non-goals

JobJitsu will **not** become the following — even if competitors do.

| Non-goal | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **A job board** | We operate on the hunt; we do not become the marketplace |
| **Cloud SaaS that holds résumés by default** | Privacy is architecture, not a toggle |
| **Subscription hostage model** | No locking career data behind paywalls |
| **Spray-and-pray autopilot** | Volume without leverage contradicts mastery |
| **Fully autonomous “we land the job for you”** | Agent prepares; human owns send and career claims |
| **Urgency / streak / guilt growth loops** | Anxiety is not a growth metric |
| **Employer-side surveillance or ranking of candidates** | We serve the seeker’s craft, not hiring funnels against them |
| **Purple-glow AI chatbot novelty** | Precision tool, not hype demo |
| **Hostile “crush recruiters / beat ATS at all costs” framing** | Gentle redirection; employers aren’t enemies |
| **Mobile-first social feed of jobs** | Desktop-native OS; focus over infinite scroll |
| **Guaranteed interviews or offers** | Honest claims only |
| **Advertising or resale of user career data** | Betrays the dojo |

If a proposal requires a non-goal, it is out of vision — redesign or reject.

## Product Principles

Decision filters for every module, roadmap item, and experience. Aligned with [PRODUCT_PHILOSOPHY.md](./PRODUCT_PHILOSOPHY.md) and the brand system.

### 1. Privacy is the platform
On-device by default. Outbound only when the user acts. Visible Local LLM / trust chrome is part of the product, not a footnote.

### 2. Sovereignty over convenience theater
User owns preferences, pause, approve, and send. Convenience never removes the signal.

### 3. Leverage over volume
Optimize for well-aimed throws and fit — not vanity send counts or “50 today” quotas.

### 4. One calm operating system
Modules share voice, visual language, and mental model. No cockpit sprawl; one job per view.

### 5. Agent as belt, not leash
Automation increases readiness and technique. It does not replace judgment or invent urgency.

### 6. Craft over choreography
Celebrate consistency and skill. Treat setbacks as information. No confetti for routine saves; no shame for rest.

### 7. Open trust
Open-source inspectability and local execution beat marketing promises.

### 8. Native, light, fast
Desktop tool energy: crisp, keyboard-capable, respectful of the machine. Dark mode first; Midnight + teal identity holds.

### 9. Honest AI
Help draft, tailor, queue, and remind. Never claim hiring outcomes the product cannot promise.

### 10. Brand as product behavior
If it wouldn’t fit calm confidence — Leverage Belt, teal flap sacred, metaphor as seasoning — it doesn’t ship as JobJitsu.

### Principle checklist (use before committing roadmap work)

| Ask | Must answer |
|-----|-------------|
| Does this keep data on-device by default? | Yes, or redesign |
| Does this increase leverage or only volume? | Leverage |
| Does the user retain control of outbound actions? | Always |
| Does this reduce anxiety or manufacture it? | Reduce |
| Does this deepen the Career OS or distract into a side quest? | Deepen |
| Would a black belt say this calmly? | If no, rewrite or cut |
| Does it violate a non-goal? | If yes, stop |
